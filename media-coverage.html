Hong Kong Family Offices Take Center Stage: Nice Talent Asset Management Invited to Speak at Abu Dhabi Global Family Office Investment Forum

Nice Talent Asset Management joined Global Convergence Week Abu Dhabi, where Head of Investment Nelson Ngan delivered a keynote on Hong Kong family offices going global and cross-border asset allocation.

Read the Full Story →
November 24, 2025 | Press Release

From November 17-20, 2025, the "Global Convergence Week," hosted by the Global CSR Foundation, was grandly held at the iconic Emirates Palace in Abu Dhabi. Nice Talent Asset Management Limited, was invited to attend this international event and, as a representative of Hong Kong family offices, delivered a speech during the "Global Family Office Investment Forum." They engaged in discussions with global family office leaders, ultra-high-net-worth investors, and thoughtful leaders from the Middle East, Asia, Europe, and the Americas on the future of family wealth management.

"True global leadership stems from responsibility and vision. Family capital should not only pursue growth, but more importantly, drive the world to develop for the better." — Jing Zhao Cesarone, Global CSR Foundation CEO & Global Convergence Week Chairperson

This four-day Global Convergence Week, themed "Innovation, Cultural Diplomacy, and Shared Prosperity," brought together global leaders from business, culture, and technology sectors, aiming to foster cross-sectoral and cross-border collaborative progress. Among its segments, the "Global Family Office Investment Forum & Project Roadshow Days" held on November 17-18 on the theme "Legacy, Innovation, Impact: Shaping the Future of Global Family Wealth." It focused on key areas such as private market and direct investment strategies, impact investing and ESG, family governance and next-generation leadership, and technological innovation and digital transformation.

Nice Talent at Global Convergence Week Abu Dhabi, hosted by the Global CSR Foundation (17–20 November 2025).

As the invited Hong Kong family office representative, Nice Talent Asset Management Limited's Head of Investment, Nelson Ngan, delivered a keynote speech titled "How Global Financial Evolution Brings New Opportunities to Family Offices." He elaborated on the profound impact of changes in the global financial landscape on family wealth management from multiple dimensions, as well as the unique advantages of Asia, particularly Hong Kong as an international financial centre, in capturing global opportunities and wealth succession. His speech resonated strongly with the attendees, with several international family office principals expressing interest in deeper cooperation after the forum.

Nice Talent Asset Management Limited was founded in 2018, headquartered in Hong Kong, Nice Talent Asset Management Limited has successively established branches in Singapore and Dubai, dedicated to providing tailored wealth management services for high-net-worth clients. The company is licensed by the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission (CE Number BMR849) to conduct Dealing in Securities (Type 1 regulated activity), Advising on Securities (Type 4 regulated activity), and Asset Management (Type 9 regulated activity).

Nice Talent Asset Management Limited is highly recognized for its professional expertise and innovative service model. In 2024, it was honored as the "Best External Asset Management" by Hong Kong Commercial Times, "Top Financial Asset Management Provider in APAC" by Financial Services Review, and "Best EAM of the Year" by Business Innovator. Furthermore, it has been named "Top FIM/IAM Partner" by Bank of Singapore for five consecutive years (2020–2024), underscoring the deep trust placed in the firm by both industry peers and clients.

Engaging with hosts and international partners during Global Convergence Week Abu Dhabi.

As a member of the Elite EAM Alliance, Nice Talent Asset Management Limited is integrated into the leading external asset management (EAM) ecosystem in North Asia, established by the Family Office Association Hong Kong (FOAHK). This alliance comprises 15 to 20 external asset management firms, representing over 500 SFC license holders and a combined industry scale of $100 billion. Nice Talent Asset Management Limited has established multi-year partnerships with over 10 private banks in Greater China, Singapore, and Switzerland, including renowned financial institutions such as Bank of Singapore, BOC International Securities, DBS Private Bank, UBS, Standard Chartered Private Bank, and Bank of East Asia. It has also set up a representative office in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) in the UAE, forming a strategic partnership with Emirates NBD, making it one of the first Hong Kong-headquartered external asset managers to enter the UAE market.

Looking Ahead: Diversified Family Office Services for Global Chinese Families

Looking forward, beyond traditional asset management, Nice Talent Asset Management Limited plans to expand into diversified family office services. Simultaneously, the company will continue to deepen its business presence in Singapore, the Middle East, and other regions, offering clients greater flexibility in wealth management and serving more global high-net-worth clients.

This trip to Abu Dhabi not only signifies the further elevation of Nice Talent Asset Management Limited's influence on the international stage but also highlights the professional expertise and innovative capabilities of Hong Kong's family offices in the global wealth management arena. The Nice Talent Asset Management Limited team stated: "A family office is not merely a tool for wealth management but an important vehicle for transmitting family values and driving social progress. Nice Talent Asset Management Limited will continue to uphold its vision and mission of 'acting in the best interest of every client, providing the best risk-adjusted performance, and maintaining long-term relationships with clients.' We are committed to providing exceptional wealth management services for global Chinese families and working together to create a more prosperous future."
